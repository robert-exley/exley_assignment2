Sugar, Sugar How You Get So High:
Calcuate How Much Sugar Is In Your Starbucks

by Robert Exley

Millions of Americans go to Starbucks everday for coffee. (Photo: Starbucks)

With over 11,000 stores in the U.S. alone, millions of Americans turn to Starbucks everyday to get their morning boost, but some may not realize the amount of sugar hiding in their favorite drinks.

While coffee and tea alone contain no sugar, many elect to add flavors and sweeteners that can come loaded with sugar. The biggest offenders are Frappuccino’s, some of which can exceed the daily recommendation in one serving.

According to the American Heart Association, the daily-recommended sugar intake is 37.5 grams for men and 25 grams for women. The AHA identified “sugary drinks” as one of the largest contributors to rising levels of obesity in society.
